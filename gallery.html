Who are we?

We are Dublin's leading boxing academy, we have the best coaches in Ireland who are AIBA qualified. We have coached athletes with no experience to winning All Ireland Championships and some have even went to the European and World Championship sanctioned by the IABA!

We also have Boxercise if you want to train like a boxer without the boxing!
